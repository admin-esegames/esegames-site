ÉSÈGAMES at Creative Bloc Carnival 2024

2024-10-05

Lagos, Nigeria – The Creative Bloc Carnival 2024, held on October 5th at Muri Okunola Park, was a vibrant after-party for the Africacomicade Gamathon 2024. Sponsored by Africacomicade, Mavin Records, and The Artists Commune, this event was a celebration of creativity, fun, and games.

Read more

A Fun-Filled After-Party

The Creative Bloc Carnival transformed Muri Okunola Park into a lively hub of entertainment and engagement. Unlike traditional showcases, this event focused on fun and interactive experiences. Attendees enjoyed a variety of activities, including a raffle draw by LG, where lucky winners took home OLED TVs, Boom Players, and other exciting prizes. Indomie also delighted many with their giveaways.

Competitions and Prizes

The carnival featured competitions targeted at TikTokers, with some participants winning over ₦200,000, while others took home ₦100,000 and other prizes. These competitions added an extra layer of excitement and engagement, drawing in a diverse crowd of content creators and enthusiasts.

Cinema and Art Exploration

A unique highlight was the cinema experience, where attendees wore headphones to enjoy movies in an open-air setting. The park was adorned with art installations, providing a visual feast for attendees to explore and enjoy. This blend of visual and interactive elements created a truly immersive experience.

Brand Engagement and Food Adventures

Many brands attended the carnival, offering prizes to those who engaged with their games and activities. The event was also a culinary adventure, with food vendors offering a wide array of dishes to tantalize taste buds. The variety of food options ensured that there was something for everyone, making the event a delightful experience for all attendees.

Music and Games

The carnival was filled with music, creating a festive atmosphere that kept the energy high throughout the day. Numerous games were available for attendees to engage with, ensuring that there was never a dull moment. The combination of music, games, and interactive experiences made the Creative Bloc Carnival a memorable after-party for the Gamathon.

Conclusion

The Creative Bloc Carnival 2024 was a resounding success, offering a dynamic and engaging platform for fun and creativity. For ÉSÈGAMES, the event was an invaluable opportunity to connect with the community and enjoy the vibrant atmosphere. As ÉSÈGAMES continues to grow and innovate, their participation in events like the Creative Bloc Carnival underscores their commitment to excellence and creativity.

ÉSÈGAMES Shines at Africacomicade Gamathon 2024

2024-10-04

Lagos, Nigeria – The Africacomicade Gamathon 2024, held from September 30 to October 5, was a landmark event for the African video games and digital media industry. Among the many highlights of the event was the participation of ÉSÈGAMES, a rising star in the gaming community.

Read more

Industry Day at Odeya Event Centre: A Showcase of Innovation

On Friday, October 4th, the Industry Day took place at the Odeya Event Centre, bringing together game studios from across Africa to showcase their latest projects. Delegates from various parts of the continent gathered to witness the creativity and innovation driving the African gaming industry. ÉSÈGAMES proudly presented their games, including the much-anticipated AGBA RUNNER, during this vibrant showcase.

Ark Pitch Fellowship: A Platform for Visionaries

ÉSÈGAMES also participated in the Ark Pitch Fellowship, pitching AGBA RUNNER to the public, including delegates and industry experts. This opportunity allowed ÉSÈGAMES to connect with other developers, gain valuable feedback, and explore potential collaborations. The experience was invaluable, providing insights and connections that will undoubtedly propel their projects forward.

Interactive Gaming Experiences

The Gamathon featured a dedicated gaming section where attendees could play a variety of games. Additionally, the event included VR and AR experiences, allowing participants to engage with cutting-edge technology and test games from numerous studios. These interactive sessions were a hit, drawing large crowds and fostering a sense of community among gamers and developers alike.

Insightful Sessions and Keynote Talks

The event also hosted several insightful sessions and talks. A standout moment was the keynote presentation by Jide Martin, the CEO of Comic Republic, who not only shared their inspiring journey from humble beginnings to signing a deal with Universal Studios but also presented on creating Nigeria’s first 2D anime and games. The talk emphasized the importance of protecting intellectual property (IP) and provided practical advice for aspiring creators.

Networking and Refreshments

Throughout the event, attendees enjoyed refreshments while networking with peers and industry leaders. This informal setting was perfect for introducing ÉSÈGAMES and their impressive lineup of games, including Daruuu, AGBA RUNNER, and Project Genesis. The positive reception and interest from attendees highlighted the growing recognition of ÉSÈGAMES in the industry.

Conclusion

The Africacomicade Gamathon 2024 was a resounding success for ÉSÈGAMES. The event not only provided a platform to showcase their innovative games but also offered invaluable opportunities for learning, networking, and growth. As ÉSÈGAMES continues to make strides in the gaming industry, their participation in events like the Gamathon underscores their commitment to excellence and innovation.
